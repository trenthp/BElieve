The Reason Why

Brooke Ellefsen is a dedicated Wife, Mother of 5, Soccer Coach, Youth Leader, Marathon Runner, a willing volunteer, and her coconut rice is seriously divine! She works hard, plays hard and loves hard. There is no one quite like her.


Last week, Brooke was diagnosed with Stage IV Metastatic Lung Cancer. While this is devastating, her optimism is consistently shining through and her strength is a blessing and comfort to all.


Her dear friends are hosting a Fund Run on October 22. It will take place up on the 5 Mile Prairie at Prairie View Elementary. Registration is $10, starts at 2:30 and there will also be t-shirts available for purchase. We hope to see you there!

Thank you for all of your support during this difficult time. We hope to see you there!
